## How to Install

1. Download the `app-debug.apk` file from the link above.
2. Transfer it to your Android device if downloaded on PC.
3. On your Android device, go to **Settings → Security → Install Unknown Apps** and allow your browser or file manager.
4. Open the APK file and tap **Install**.
5. Once installed, open **CRIX** from your app drawer.

> **Requires Android 5.0 (Lollipop) or higher.**

---

## Sign In

CRIX uses **Google Sign-In** for authentication. When you open the app for the first time, you will be prompted to sign in with your Google account.

- Your data is securely tied to your Google account.
- All transactions are synced to your personal cloud storage in real time.
- Signing out or switching devices retains all your data — just sign in again with the same Google account.

---

## Features

- Log income and expenses with categories and dates.
- Visual analytics and spending breakdowns.
- Set and monitor daily or monthly spending limits.
- Full transaction history with edit and delete support.
- Cloud sync across sessions via your Google account.
